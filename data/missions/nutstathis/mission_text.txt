Location: Outskirts of the Hydorah system
Date: 197.21.02

While the Tri Tachyon Corporation and Shadowyards Heavy Industries relations are cordial, if cool, they are only tepidly aligned at most.  Both factions have, under the right circumstances, come to blows in the past, typically over contested Techmining operations.

A small Shadowyards sponsored techmining group finds their fleet unexpectedly confronted with a band of TriTachyon privateers insisting they have prior claim to the site; knowing this claim to be false and unwilling to surrender her claim without a fight, the commander of small flotilla orders her fleet to take up defensive positions.
