Location: An unexplored system
Date: 209.06.08

While patrolling through unknown space, a Shadowyards detachment detects an unidentified raiding force attempting to slip into Cooperative controlled space undetected.

While not a significant threat directly, the hostile force possesses sufficient weght to seriously threaten shipping within SHI's zone of control; not entirely certain who she's up against, the Commander nevertheless orders her unit to chart an intercept course.
